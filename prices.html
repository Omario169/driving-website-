Pass Plus

Course Price

The Pass Plus course is a scheme aimed at new drivers, although anyone with a full UK driving licence can take the course.

The course has been designed by the DVSA (Driver & Vehicle Licensing Agency), vehicle insurers and the driving instruction industry.

The purpose of the course is to help new drivers reach a safer and more competent level of driving (not all driving instructors offer the course).
