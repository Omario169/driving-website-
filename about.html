About us

I am qualified as a DSA Approved Driving Instructor and have also passed the IAM Advanced Test and the Diamond Special Assessment. My goal is to provide tuition to enable you to pass the practical driving test and become a safe, confident driver. Your lessons will be conducted one to one and will be tailored to your individual requirements and skills.

My prices are very competitive and I offer block booking discounts. If you want to learn how to drive quickly but still become a safe driver, why not do an intensive course? Available over 7 days, 14 days or 28 days please get in touch for more information. As well as helping you pass the practical test, I will help you with the theory exam and can provide books and DVDs to assist you. I also offer Pass Plus to give you even more confidence behind the wheel. Some councils may offer subsidies on the Pass Plus fee and most insurance companies offer a discount for those who possess a Pass Plus qualification.
