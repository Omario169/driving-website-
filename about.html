A little more about me, Abul

Thanks for visiting my Driving School website, here's a bit about me and why I became a Driving Instructor...

Having worked and gained knowledge in the transport industry for over 28 years, I decided I would like to give some of my road safety expertise back to new drivers.

I've seen road users from many different perspectives, having riden motorcycles and driven cars, vans and small trucks (7.5 ton) - up to class one and two large goods vehicles.

Driving instruction was the perfect option for me because I like to contribute a safe, accident free environment for all road users to enjoy with confidence and courtesy, even the most nervous of drivers.

My other full time job has been being a single parent for 10 years; raising my son and daughter.

My interests include classic cars and most genres of vehicle racing - especially truck racing

If you're thinking about driving lessons, please contact Ashtons Driving School. I'd be happy to help you become a good and safe driver for life.

DSA Diamond Approved Instructor
